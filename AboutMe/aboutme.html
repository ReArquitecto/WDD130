About Me

I've lived my whole life in a tiny town in Colorado called Rye. There my dad started a buisness called Piano Marvel that teaches people how to play the piano with online assessments and tutorials. I've been a part of that project my whole life and I've had a front row seat in seeing Piano Marvel grow. That's my job right now. I work with and manage the software development team. The same team just remade the music player on the church website. My involvment in the church has long been one of the most important aspects of my life. Like just about everyone, I've always struggled with my own feelings of inadequacy but I work hard to change so I can contribute more to Heavenly Father's plan for his children. One problem that gets to me is that I have a very reserved personality. There are a lot of people that openly express themselves in front of others. That's not something I've ever developed. I'm calm and reserved and it can be a problem sometimes. One of my biggest fears is that I might not be able to achieve all my goals. I see so many limitations on who I can become. I see advantages and disadantages to my reserved personality. I want to learn to be less shy and improve my ability to communicate and be a positive influence.
